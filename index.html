Gaia Reset

Gaia Reset is a project focused on raising awareness
about the consequences of environmental neglect.
It presents realistic themes in a dystopian setting,
where most of humanity has taken refuge in space,
leaving only a few survivors in a designated
environmental reserve.

You take on the role of Vladislav, one of the
survivors, whose main goal is to help others
in the reserve with their problems and, if
possible, find a solution to their situation.

This game was developed over a year and a half
and underwent numerous changes and challenges along
the way. Nevertheless, my team and I managed to
complete the project in a way that I consider
satisfactory.
